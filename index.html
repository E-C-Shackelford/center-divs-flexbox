I am a visual artist and apsiring front-end developer and visual designer based in Portland, Oregon. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies from Marylhurst University in 2011 and the concentration of my B.A. was self-designed, primarily incorporating fine art and psychology. As a visual artist, I specialize in oil-based colored pencil and graphite, and my work primarily centers around pet, animal and human portraiture; female empowerment; inner magic; and imaginative realism.

I am passionate about creativity and movement! I love creating visual art, spending time out in nature, plants and gardening, running, fitness, music, reading, and learning about front-end development and visual design.

My passion for creativity, visual communication, the arts, interdisciplinary studies, creative problem solving; the relationship between art, people, communication, and technology; and my fascination with the ways in which technology expands the landscape of creativity, innovation, and communication, led me to not only becoming a visual artist, but to also pursuing my studies in front-end development, visual design, and user experience design.
